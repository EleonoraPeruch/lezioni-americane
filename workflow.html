The Workflow

Here are the steps that I followed during the realisation of the project

Semantic Analysis

The first step has been a close reading of the lecture and the analysis of other textual sources to better define the initial idea concerning what is “unsaid”: as a result, the four semantic levels of analysis have been defined. I decided to concentrate the analysis on the first lecture, due to its prestige and its being the first.

Text Encoding

The txt file of the lecture has been encoded in XML using Atom. I established an interpretation group and then I used TEI for the markup.

XSLT to HTML

Once the text was marked-up with all the necessary elements, I used XSLT to transform it into an HTML file, including the information concerning the four semantic levels previously defined.

XQUERY to HTML

I used some XQUERY functions to extract some other information from the xml file and get a further insight into the text of the lecture.

Javascript to get insights

Javascript has been deployed to highlight the distribution of the four semantic levels in the text and their predominance in terms of percentage.
